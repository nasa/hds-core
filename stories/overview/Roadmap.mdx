## Planned for future releases

After v1.0, HDS Core will expand to cover more of the Horizon Design System.

**Navigation** — Header, footer, secondary navigation bar, and menu variants. Top priority for the first post-v1.0 release.

**Cards & layout** — Cards, image with caption, gallery thumbnails, grid utilities

**Interactive components** — Dropdown menus, modals, tooltips, date picker, search, table of contents

**Status & feedback** — Banner (government compliance bar), alerts, status indicators, loaders, steppers

**Media** — Audio player, video player, carousels

**Extended form elements** — File upload, sliders, toggles

**Data visualization** — Chart color palettes and accessibility guidance are already available in Foundations. Future releases may add chart component wrappers.

These components require JavaScript, additional design work, or creative director review beyond the current scope. Priorities will be based on community input — check the [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/nasa/hds-core/discussions) to see what's being considered and to share your needs.

## Additional components

All USWDS components work with HDS Core theming out of the box — colors, typography, and spacing apply automatically. Components listed above will receive dedicated HDS styling and documentation. USWDS components not listed here (button groups, collections, side navigation, etc.) continue to work with the default USWDS appearance, themed to HDS.
